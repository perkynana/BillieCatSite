About me

Living the dream

Hey there, I'm Billie 🐱

I want to start off by thanking you for taking the time to vist my page, it really means a lot.

So, a little about me. Born September 2016, I started out life as an SPCA special, not knowing where I was going to be from one day to the next or if anyone would ever take a chance on me, I guess only time would tell.
A number of months had passed, including Christmas, still no one chose me. I was always asking myself "What is wrong with me?".

Then in Januay, 2017, 2 humans did a double take. I thought to myself, this is it, this is my moment. I sat there in my glass case of emotions, on my bestest behaviour, looking over at them, trying to make sure that they looked at no other cat.

Then just like that, I got them. Hook, line and sinker!
We've been together ever since, it really is...
