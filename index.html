Because I'm worth it!

Hey there, I'm Billie, thanks for stopping by and taking the time to get to know a little about me.

I started out in life as an SPCA special, until one day in January 2017, 2 amazing humans stopped and stared at me through the glass pane of my, what I like to call, box from hell. It's OK, I wasn’t alone, I was accompanied by my sister, who at the time was a lot more energetic than I, and far more attention seeking than what was needed!

However, that didn’t stop these folk taking a liking to me and asking to have a hold to size me up!

I didn’t have much to offer in terms of excitement at the time, in fact I was feeling pretty meh on this day and after a few minutes with me, they decided to put me back. I wasn't sure if they were interested anymore but lo and behold, I was placed on hold. Rumour has it they took a liking, but were concerned about my well being, so they asked if they could come back to check on me later that day.

I didn’t think they would come back to visit me, but just before the doors were about to close (talking about leaving it to the last minute here), there they stood! They reached in and started to check on me. Again, much like earlier in the day, I was still not 100%. After being checked over by the vet, I was taken away a put in isolation.

Days went by, and no one came to see me, I thought that that was it, I was going to be all by myself from here on out. What I didn't know though, was that the 2 awesome peeps I mentioned earlier, had already agreed to take me home, once I was well enough to leave.

So, what has happened since then you ask?

Rather quickly, I made myself at home.

  • I claimed my spot on the couch
  • I claimed my spot on the bed
  • I located some climbing apparatus

After settling in, I started to go on a few adventures

  • I met a brother from another mother
  • I travelled to outer space
  • I even learnt to swim
This site has been created purely for the fun of it.

We just wanted people to see just how cute our furbaby is. She brightens up our lives everyday, purely by being the crazy critter that she is.
