Intro

Hey there, I am Billie

I want to start off by thanking you for taking the time to vist my page, it really means a lot to me.

So, a little about me. Born September 2016, I started out life as an SPCA special, not knowing where I was going to be from one day to the next or if anyone would ever take a chance on me, I guess only time would tell.
A number of months had passed, including Christmas, still no one chose me. I was always asking myself "What is wrong with me?".

Then towards the end of Januay, 2017, 2 humans did a double take. I thought to myself, this is it, this is my moment to shine. I sat there, in my glass case of emotions, on my bestest behaviour, looking over at them, trying to make sure they looked at no other.



Then just like that, I got them. Hook, line and sinker!

We've been together ever since, I like to think of it as ...
